Project Panda

Let’s face it: the panda bear is a genetic abomination. It has a near-nutritionless diet and steadfastly refuses to mate on all but two days each year. If we really want this species (or anything like it) to survive, the time has come for drastic intervention.

Our Mission

Even as Charles Darwin writhes in his grave, humanity is trying its darndest to keep panda bears alive.

Project Panda was founded as an alternative to these traditional conservation efforts. Rather than attempting to preserve the panda bear as it exists today, Project Panda intends to splice panda DNA with that of other animal species. As much as everyone seems to adore these hapless mammals, we believe we can do better.
